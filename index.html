How it works

A simple, recurring loop

Each employee picks the metric that fits their life and sets a goal on a weekly or bi-weekly cadence. Hit it, the payout triggers. Miss it, no penalty — it just resets next cycle.

Employee

A mobile app to connect a wearable, pick a metric, watch progress against the goal, and see payouts land.

Employer

A dashboard showing total payouts, how many employees are hitting goals, and how engagement trends over time.

Pact

Why it's different

A neutral layer built on trust

Your health data stays private

Employees don't want their employer holding raw health data — and they shouldn't have to. Pact sits in the middle as a neutral third party. The employer sees that a payout is owed, never someone's heart-rate history.

Works with any wearable

Everyone has a different favorite, and no one wants to switch. A single-brand program caps its own adoption. Pact is device-agnostic by design — the neutral layer that works no matter what's on your wrist.

The bigger bet

Every healthy person we keep engaged with a wearable is another stream of continuous biometric data — and that data is becoming genuinely predictive of disease. By paying people to stay engaged, Pact isn't just a benefit: it helps build the substrate that makes population-scale prediction possible. Primary prevention because you're rewarded for it, and secondary prevention because the data catches what's coming early.
