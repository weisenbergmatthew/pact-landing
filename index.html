How it works

A simple weekly loop, built to last.

Each employee chooses the goal that fits their life and a weekly target. Hit it by the end of the week and the payout lands. Miss it — no penalty; it simply resets. The reward recurs every week, which is exactly what sustained behavior change requires.

  1. 01

    Connect a wearable

    Employees link the device they already own. Pact reads only what's needed to confirm a goal was met.

  2. 02

    Set a weekly goal

    They pick the metric and target that fits their life, on a single weekly cadence everyone understands.

  3. 03

    Hit it, get rewarded

    Progress tracks automatically. Clear the goal by week's end and the payout lands — then it repeats.

One definition of "healthy" doesn't fit everyone. Pact meets each person where they are.

Why it's different

A neutral layer, built on employee trust.

Health data stays private

Employees don't want their employer holding raw health data — a rational concern Pact is built around. As a neutral third party, we confirm only whether a goal was met. The employer sees that a payout is owed, never someone's heart-rate history.

Works with any wearable

Everyone has a favorite device, and no one wants to switch. A single-brand program caps its own adoption. Pact is device-agnostic by design — the neutral layer that works no matter what's on the wrist. That neutrality is the moat.
