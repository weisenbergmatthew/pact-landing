How it works

A simple weekly loop that builds healthier habits.

Each employee picks a health goal that fits their life. Complete it by the end of the week and the payout is theirs. Miss it — no penalty; it simply resets the following week.

01

Connect a wearable

Employees link the wearable they already own. Pact reads only what's needed to confirm a goal was met.

02

Set a weekly goal

Each week, employees pick the health goal that fits their priorities.

03

Hit it, get rewarded

Progress is tracked automatically by their wearable. Complete the goal by week's end and the payout is theirs. Then it repeats the following week.

Why it works

A neutral layer, built on employee trust.

Health data stays private

Employees don't want their employer holding their wearable data — a rational concern Pact is built around. As a neutral third party, we confirm only whether a goal was met. The employer sees that a payout is owed, never someone's heart-rate history.

Works with any wearable

Everyone has a favorite device, and no one wants to switch. A single-brand program caps its own adoption. Pact is device-agnostic by design — the neutral layer that works no matter what's on the wrist.
